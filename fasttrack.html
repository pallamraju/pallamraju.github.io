Overview

FastTrack is designed for both business and consumer customers, it serves as the basis for Quicksilver Catalogue. It's Ideal for the business who are into automating their networks. FastTrack enacts as central point of data source that can be used to feed information such as Demo Connect to accelerate the presales process, Business Calculators to perform financial functions, Collateral to collect media used to support the sales of Quicksilver, and Partner Central to search for Quicksilver partners within the ecosystem that have specialized knowledge and expertise.
